- Developing an interactive ranch map (Phase 1) with clickable locations and basic interactions.
- Implementing a hybrid shop system (Phase 1 refinement) with karma coin purchases, progressive inventory, and coming soon items.
- Adding deeper RPG mechanics (Phase 2): XP gain, leveling, trait selection, and enhanced inventory, including farming actions in the Open Pasture and animal treats in shops.
- Implementing an AI-driven quest system (Phase 3): Main and side quests, affinity system for NPCs, procedural events, and branching dialogues.
